Welcome to My Sculpture Portfolio: A Ninth Grader's Journey in Form

Hello! I'm a ninth-grade student, and this website is dedicated to my journey in the art of **sculpture**. I created this space to share my experiments with clay, plaster, and other materials, and to connect with others who appreciate the three-dimensional art form.

My work primarily focuses on blending **traditional figurative techniques**—understanding anatomy and proportion—with **modern abstract concepts** where form and movement are the main subjects. I am currently fascinated by how texture can communicate emotion.

My Goal: Seeking Constructive Criticism

As a student, growth is my primary objective. I built this site specifically to gain **constructive criticism and comments** from visitors like you. Every piece here, from quick studies to larger projects, is a learning opportunity. Please feel free to explore my gallery and projects, and use the contact information on the "About Me" page to share your thoughts on the composition, technique, or emotional impact of my work.
